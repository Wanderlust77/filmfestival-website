Stewarding at Festivals

Stewarding is a fantastic way to attend Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival, offering a chance to learn new skills, meet new people and see some of your favourite movies without the cost.

We’re on the hunt for friendly, personable event stewards for our film festival and possibly throughout the year. You’ll be helping people get from A-B and making sure attendees enjoy themselves so you’ll ideally love spending time with others and have a fantastic sense of humour to boot. In return you’ll get an unforgettable experience seeing behind the scenes of your favourite festivals, with plenty of time to enjoy the event itself and an opportunity to make new, likeminded friends.

Think it sounds like something you might be interested in? Read below or apply here.

What is stewarding?

Stewards are what keep festivals running; helping control crowds, directing attendees to the right location and creating a happy, vibrant atmosphere. As a festival steward, you migh t be needed in a multitude of different jobs; from helping people park safely, to giving information and directing attendees around the site.

What stewarding jobs are available?

  • Stewarding
  • Information Point Personnel
  • Event Ambassador
  • Car Park Assistant
  • Disabled/Campervan Area Steward

You may also be asked to take on additional responsibilities or help out elsewhere across the event.
